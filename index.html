What is AIP?

AIP or Auto Immune Protocol is a version of the Paleo Diet, it is believed to benefit individuals suffering from automimmune diseases by eliminating certain foods that cause inflamation.

Read more about AIP

Foods to Eat / Avoid

Avoid

  • eggs
  • grains
  • dairy
  • nuts
  • nightshades: tomatoes, eggplants...
  • alcohol

Eat

  • grass-fed, wild, pasture-raised meats
  • organ meats
  • wild-caught fish
  • most vegetables
  • fruits (2-5 servings a day)
  • oils:avocado, olive, coconut, ghee...
  • fermented foods
